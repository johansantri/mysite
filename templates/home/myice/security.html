At MyICE, protecting your personal information and ensuring the security of our platform is a top priority. This Security Policy outlines the measures we take to safeguard your data.

1. Data Protection

We use industry-standard encryption protocols (such as SSL/TLS) to protect data transmitted between your device and our servers. All sensitive information, including passwords and payment details, is encrypted and stored securely.

2. Access Control

Access to user data is strictly limited to authorized personnel only. We implement role-based access controls and regularly review permissions to ensure data privacy.

3. Secure Infrastructure

Our servers and infrastructure are hosted in secure data centers with robust physical and network security measures. Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments are conducted to maintain system integrity.

4. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials and should use strong, unique passwords. We encourage enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) where available.

5. Incident Response

In the event of a data breach or security incident, MyICE has a dedicated response team to quickly address the issue, notify affected users, and take corrective actions.

6. Third-Party Security

We work only with trusted third-party service providers who comply with stringent security standards to protect your data.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Security Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with the date of revision.

8. Contact Us

For any questions or concerns regarding security, please contact us at support@ini.icei.ac.id.
